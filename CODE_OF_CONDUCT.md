# Code of Conduct – Open Pleasure Labs

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

**This is a sex-positive, kink-positive, and body-positive space.** All anatomical discussions are clinical, design-oriented, or educational. No fetish shaming. No gatekeeping.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Respecting privacy – no sharing of others' data, photos, or personal information
- Clear consent before any private messaging or offline interaction

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Sexualized language or imagery of *specific individuals* without consent
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Unsolicited explicit content or "dick pics" in any community channel
- Ageism or exclusion based on being 18+ (this is an adult space only)
- Advocating for or encouraging illegal activity

## Age Requirement

**You must be 18 years or older** to participate in this community. Anyone found to be under 18 will be banned immediately. This is non-negotiable.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces (GitHub, Discord, Reddit, Matrix, mailing lists, events) and also when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community maintainers at `conduct@openpleasure.org` (placeholder – set up a real email).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Community maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate.

## Enforcement Guidelines

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| First offense – minor (e.g., off-topic explicit content) | Warning + message deletion |
| Second offense – minor | 7-day mute |
| First offense – severe (harassment, doxxing, underage) | Immediate permanent ban |
| Repeated offenses | Permanent ban |
